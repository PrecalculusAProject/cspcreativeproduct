IB Group 4 CSP – Interdisciplinary Project

Exploring How School Design Affects Human-Environment Interactions

Statement of Inquiry

To what extent does the design of North Atlanta High School contribute to positive interactions between human society and the environment?

Interdisciplinary Connection

This project brings together insights from Physics, Biology, and Sports & Exercise Health Science (SEHS). Each discipline conducted an experiment that contributes to a unified understanding of how the physical design of our school environment affects student well-being, behavior, and environmental interaction.
